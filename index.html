Contortionist Show in Grand Rapids

Oct 16, 2022

The Contortionist is a progressive metal band from Indianapolis, IN. In mid-2022, they set out on a tour on which they would be playing Exoplanet and Language, their first and third albums respectively, front-to-back. These albums feature many of their best songs including: Flourish, Advent, Vessel, Exoplanet I, II, and III, Language I and II, and Thrive. This tour had two dates in Michigan, the first being Wednesday, September 28th in Detroit, MI at St. Andrew's Hall and the other being the following Monday, October 1st in Grand Rapids, MI at Elevation. However, on the 29th of September after having played the date in Detroit, they announced that they would be cancelling the Grand Rapids date. This led to the outrage of many (two, that we know of) of their Michigan local fans. The fans had planned to attend the Grand Rapids date to avoid the risk of injury at St. Andrew's Hall after the floor at said venue having nearly collapsed during a show in March 2022. The band may have avoided some of the outrage had they announced the cancellation several days prior to at least inform their Michigan fanbase that there would only be a single date in Michigan for the tour. The failure to do so has left several fans devestated, as the band is unlikely to ever perform most of the songs on the aforementioned albums live ever again, especially in the same manner.

Darkmoon Card Greatness

Oct 16, 2022

Darkmoon Card: Greatness is a legendary piece of equipment in World of Warcraft: Wrath of The Lich King Classic. It is a crafted item that is BiS (Best-in-Slot) for any class that is able to take advantage of it throughout the first 2 raid tiers of the expansion, Naxxramas (Tier 7) and Ulduar (Tier 8). Darkmoon Card: Greatness, much like all other Darkmoon Cards, is a trinket slot item. It provides +90 to a primary stat having four different versions, one each for Strength, Agility, Intellect, and Spirit. It also has a proc which provides +300 to the same stat for 15 seconds with a 45 second internal cooldown, meaning that the proc cannot occur more than once every 45 seconds, so it can be active one third of the time. Though the item is very famous, it is also infamous. Its infamy derives from the expense to acquire it. There is a long process to crafting it and some lightly time-gated methods to acquiring it. The crafting is done by a scribe who mills herbs from Northrend to create Azure and Icy pigments, which are then converted to Ink of the Sea and Snowfall Ink, respectively. By combining 3 Ink of the Sea, 6 Snowfall Ink, and 3 Eternal Life (another item created by gathering herbs) on a piece of Resilient Parchment, the scribe can create a random Darkmoon Card of the North. There are 32 possible outcomes for this. The cards have a face value of Ace through 8 and there are four decks to which they belong: Undeath, Prisms, Chaos, and the deck which creates a Darkmoon Card: Greatness, Nobles. By combining one of each face value of the same type of card, you create the corresponding deck which can then be handed in at the Darkmoon Faire. However, the Darkmoon Faire is only active one week out of the month, so naturally the price of the item goes up while it is not available, as no new DMCGs can be created.
